**Target Audience and Purpose:**

This textbook is designed for undergraduate-level engineering students who need to understand probability and statistics in their native Turkish language. The book aims to provide an easily comprehensible resource covering fundamental topics in probability theory and statistics used across all engineering disciplines. The content is adapted from the authors' previous work titled "Probability Theory and Stochastic Processes."

The book follows Voltaire's principle: "A book is only excusable insofar as it teaches something" (1765).

## Part I: Probability and Random Variables

### Chapter 1: Introduction (Giriş)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Probability Theory (Olasılık Teorisi):** Historical development originating from gambling games (dice, card games, roulette) in 17th century France
- **Random Experiments (Rastgele Deneyler):** Experiments with multiple possible outcomes that produce irregular results
- **Events and Sample Spaces (Olaylar ve Olasılık Uzayları):**
  - Sample space definition and notation (S or Ω)
  - Discrete and continuous sample spaces
  - Elementary events and outcomes
- **Probability Axioms (Olasılık Aksiyomları):** Fundamental axioms defining probability on abstract sets
- **Conditional Probability (Koşullu Olasılık):**
  - Independence of two events
  - Independence of multiple events
  - Partitioned sample spaces and total probability law
  - Bayes' Theorem
- **Historical Paradoxes:** Discussion of probability paradoxes that emerged throughout historical development

### Chapter 2: Counting Techniques and Combinatorial Analysis (Sayma Teknikleri ve Kombinatuvar Analiz)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Counting Techniques (Sayma Teknikleri):**
  - Fundamental multiplication rule
  - Inclusion/exclusion principle
- **Combinatorial Analysis (Kombinatuvar Analiz):**
  - **Permutations (Permütasyonlar):** Ordered arrangements with and without replacement
  - **Combinations (Kombinasyonlar):** Unordered selections
  - **Circular Permutations (Dairesel Permütasyon)**
  - **Permutations with Repetition:** Arrangements with indistinguishable elements
  - **Combinations with Repetition:** n different elements with k-repetition combinations

**Purpose:** These combinatorial formulas are essential for counting equally likely outcomes in games and ranking problems.

### Chapter 3: Probability Distribution Functions (Olasılık Dağılım Fonksiyonları)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Random Variable Concept (Rastgele Değişken Kavramı):**
  - Definition and types of random variables
  - Discrete and continuous random variables
- **Probability Mass Function (PMF) and Cumulative Distribution Function (CDF):**
  - Probability mass functions for discrete variables
  - Cumulative distribution functions
- **Expected Value and Variance:**
  - Expected value (mean) of discrete random variables and properties
  - Variance and standard deviation
  - Markov and Chebyshev inequalities
- **Important Discrete Distributions:**
  - **Bernoulli Distribution:** Single trial experiments
  - **Binomial Distribution:** Multiple Bernoulli trials
  - **Geometric Distribution:** Waiting time distributions
  - **Poisson Distribution:** Rare event modeling
  - **Uniform Discrete Distribution:** Equal probability outcomes
- **Continuous Random Variable Distributions:**
  - Probability density function (PDF) concept
  - Expected value and variance for continuous variables
- **Important Continuous Distributions:**
  - **Uniform (Rectangular) Continuous Distribution**
  - **Exponential Distribution:** Memoryless property applications
  - **Normal (Gaussian) Distribution:** Standard normal distribution
- **Functions of Random Variables:** Transformation of random variables
- **Mathematical Tools:**
  - Moment generating functions
  - Characteristic functions

### Chapter 4: Multi-Dimensional Probability Distribution Functions (Çok-Boyutlu Olasılık Dağılım Fonksiyonları)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Joint and Marginal Distributions (Ortak ve Marjinal Dağılımlar):**
  - Joint probability mass functions
  - Joint probability density functions
  - Joint cumulative distribution functions
  - Marginal distributions from joint distributions
- **Conditional Probability Distributions (Koşullu Olasılık Dağılımları):**
  - Conditional PMF and PDF
  - Conditional mean and variance
- **Independent Random Variables (Bağımsız Rastgele Değişkenler):**
  - Independence conditions for multiple variables
- **Covariance and Correlation (Kovaryans ve Korelasyon):**
  - Covariance definition and properties
  - Correlation coefficient
  - Orthogonal random variables
- **Two-Dimensional Normal Distribution (İki-Boyutlu Normal Dağılım)**
- **N-Dimensional Random Variables (N-Boyutlu Rastgele Değişkenler):**
  - Generalization of concepts to N dimensions

### Chapter 5: Central Limit Theorem (Merkezi Limit Teoremi)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Mean and Variance of Sums of Random Variables:**
  - Properties of sums of independent random variables
- **Probability Density of Sums:**
  - Distribution of sum of independent random variables
  - Convolution operations
- **Law of Large Numbers (Büyük Sayılar Yasası):**
  - Weak law of large numbers (Khinchin Theorem)
  - Strong law of large numbers
- **Central Limit Theorem:**
  - De Moivre-Laplace Theorem
  - Normal approximation to various distributions
  - Continuity correction
- **Applications:** Demonstrating how many practical distributions can be approximated by normal distributions

## Part II: Statistics (İstatistik)

### Chapter 6: Introduction to Statistics (İstatistiğe Giriş)

**Topics Covered:**
- **What is Statistics? (İstatistik Nedir?):** Definition and purpose of statistical methods
- **Population, Sampling, and Samples (Anakütle, Örnekleme ve Örneklem):**
  - Population (Anakütle/Yığın) concepts
  - Random sampling methods
  - Sample concepts and sample size
- **Population Parameters (Anakütle Parametreleri):** Mean, variance, and other parameters
- **Distribution of Sample Mean (Örneklem Ortalamasının Dağılımı):**
  - Properties of sample mean
  - Variance of sample mean
- **Normal Approximation of Sample Mean:** Applications of CLT to sample statistics
- **Sampling Distributions (Örnekleme Dağılımı):** Understanding statistical inference foundations

### Chapter 7: Parameter Estimation (Parametre Kestirimi)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Estimation Methods:**
  - **Method of Moments (Moment Kestirimciler Metodu):** Using sample moments to estimate parameters
  - **Maximum Likelihood Estimation (Maksimum Olabilirlik Kestirimi/MLE):**
    - Likelihood functions
    - Log-likelihood optimization
  - **Bayesian Estimation (Bayes Kestirimi):**
    - Prior and posterior distributions
    - Beta distribution applications
- **Confidence Intervals (Güven Aralıkları):**
  - Concept and interpretation
  - Confidence interval length
  - Point estimates vs. interval estimates
- **Confidence Intervals for Normal Data:**
  - **Mean with Known Variance:** Using z-distribution
  - **Mean with Unknown Variance:** Using t-distribution
  - **Variance Confidence Intervals:** Using χ² (chi-square) distribution
  - **Population Proportion Confidence Intervals:** For binary data

### Chapter 8: Hypothesis Testing (Hipotez)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Terminology:**
  - Null hypothesis (H₀) and alternative hypothesis (H₁)
  - Decision-making process
  - Critical values and rejection regions
  - P-value interpretation
- **Error Types:**
  - Type I error (false positive/α)
  - Type II error (false negative/β)
  - Power of a test
  - Effect size
- **Hypothesis Tests for Population Mean:**
  - Large sample tests (using normal distribution)
  - Small sample tests (using t-distribution)
- **Hypothesis Tests for Population Variance:**
  - Using χ² (chi-square) distribution
- **Hypothesis Tests for Population Proportion:**
  - Tests for binary outcomes
- **Chi-Square Goodness of Fit Test (Ki-Kare Uygunluk Testi):**
  - Testing if data follows a theoretical distribution
- **Chi-Square Test of Independence (Ki-Kare Bağımsızlık Testi):**
  - Testing independence between categorical variables
- **Comparing Two Sample Means:**
  - Known population variances
  - Unknown population variances (pooled variance)
- **Comparing Two Population Variances:**
  - F-test for variance ratios

### Chapter 9: Linear Regression Analysis (Doğrusal Regresyon Analizi)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Simple Linear Regression (Basit Doğrusal Regresyon Analizi):**
  - Relationship between two variables
  - Mathematical modeling of relationships
- **Least Squares Estimation (Doğrusal En Küçük Kareler Kestirimi):**
  - Minimizing sum of squared errors
  - Estimating regression coefficients
  - Correlation coefficient in regression context
- **Applications:**
  - Predicting dependent variable values
  - Statistical determination of relationships

### Chapter 10: Bayesian Updating (Bayes Güncellemesi)

**Topics Covered:**
- **Bayesian Updating Concept (Bayes Güncellemesi Kavramı):**
  - Widely used in artificial intelligence and machine learning
  - Updating beliefs with new evidence
- **Key Concepts:**
  - **Prior Probability (Önsel Olasılık):** Initial beliefs before data
  - **Likelihood (Olabilirlik):** Probability of observing data given hypothesis
  - **Posterior Probability (Sonsal Olasılık):** Updated beliefs after data
  - **Belief Updates:** Systematic revision of probabilities
- **Prior and Posterior Predictive Probabilities (Önsel ve Sonsal Öngörü Olasılıkları)**
- **Bayesian Updating with Relative Probabilities (Göreceli Olasılıklarla Bayes Güncellemesi)**
- **Bayesian Updating Table:** Systematic calculation framework
- **Notational Rules and Examples:**
  - **Discrete Hypotheses with Discrete Data**
  - **Continuous Hypotheses with Discrete Data**
  - Observations and data integration

## Key Concepts Covered

### Probability Concepts

1. **Fundamental Axioms:** Kolmogorov axioms defining probability spaces
2. **Conditional Probability and Independence:** P(A|B) and independence conditions
3. **Bayes' Theorem:** Updating probabilities with new information
4. **Total Probability Law:** Computing probabilities through partitions
5. **Combinatorics:** Permutations, combinations, and counting principles
6. **Random Variables:** Discrete and continuous types
7. **Distribution Functions:** PMF, PDF, and CDF
8. **Expected Value and Variance:** Central measures of distributions
9. **Important Distributions:**
   - Discrete: Bernoulli, Binomial, Geometric, Poisson, Uniform
   - Continuous: Uniform, Exponential, Normal
10. **Multi-dimensional Distributions:** Joint, marginal, and conditional distributions
11. **Covariance and Correlation:** Measuring relationships between variables
12. **Central Limit Theorem:** Foundation for statistical inference
13. **Law of Large Numbers:** Convergence of sample means
14. **Entropy:** Information-theoretic measure of uncertainty

### Statistical Methods

1. **Population vs. Sample:** Understanding inference from samples to populations
2. **Parameter Estimation Methods:**
   - Method of Moments
   - Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE)
   - Bayesian Estimation
3. **Confidence Intervals:** Interval estimates with specified confidence levels
4. **Hypothesis Testing Framework:**
   - Null and alternative hypotheses
   - Type I and Type II errors
   - P-values and critical regions
   - Test power and effect size
5. **Specific Hypothesis Tests:**
   - Z-tests for means (large samples)
   - t-tests for means (small samples)
   - χ² tests for variance and goodness of fit
   - F-tests for comparing variances
6. **Chi-Square Tests:**
   - Goodness of fit
   - Independence testing
7. **Regression Analysis:** Linear relationships and least squares estimation
8. **Bayesian Updating:** Sequential probability revision with new data
9. **Sampling Distributions:** Understanding variability in sample statistics
10. **Distribution Tables:** Practical tools for z, t, and χ² distributions

## Special Features

### Solved Example Problems Format

The textbook's distinguishing feature is its comprehensive collection of **solved example problems** (Çözümlü Örnek Problemler). Each chapter includes numerous worked examples demonstrating:

- Step-by-step solution methods
- Application of theoretical concepts to practical problems
- Clear mathematical derivations
- Detailed explanations of reasoning

This problem-solving approach makes the book particularly valuable for self-study and exam preparation.

### Distribution Tables

The book includes essential **statistical tables** in the appendices:

- **Standard Normal Distribution Table (z-table):** For probability calculations with standard normal variables
- **t-Distribution Table:** For small-sample inference and confidence intervals
- **χ² (Chi-Square) Distribution Table:** For variance testing and goodness-of-fit tests

These tables are crucial for performing statistical calculations without computational software.

### Practical Applications

The textbook emphasizes **real-world applications** throughout:

- Engineering problems and scenarios
- Connection to modern fields (artificial intelligence, machine learning)
- Historical context and paradoxes to deepen understanding
- Examples relevant to Turkish-speaking engineering students

### Pedagogical Approach

- **Clear Definitions:** Each concept begins with precise mathematical definitions
- **Progressive Difficulty:** Content builds systematically from basic to advanced topics
- **Comprehensive Coverage:** Both theoretical foundations and practical methods
- **Turkish Language:** Fills a gap for native Turkish speakers in technical education
- **Emphasis on Understanding:** Not just formulas, but conceptual comprehension

### Structure and Organization

- **Two-Part Division:** Clear separation between probability theory and statistics
- **Logical Flow:** Topics build on previous material in a natural progression
- **Complete Coverage:** From axioms to advanced statistical methods
- **Reference Material:** Index and bibliography support further study
- **Professional Presentation:** Published by established Turkish academic publisher
